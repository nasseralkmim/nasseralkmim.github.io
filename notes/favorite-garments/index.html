Hard shell

For when it is raining or snowing. Good for cycling in the rain. Should be orange, for visibility and snow.

Arc'teryx BETA AR €400,00

GORE-TEX N 40d 3L. Nylon, 40 denier, 3 Layers.

Denier indicates the thickness of the fibers and consequently its durability and weight.

Rab Firewall €300,00

Pertex shield 3L, not gore-tex but equivalent. It can stretches and have pít zips for ventilation. Already found it for €190,00. (campz.at)

Patagonia Torrent shell €140,00

Not Gore-Tex. It is a different equivalent fabric H2No. No waterproof zips, it uses flaps. It has pit zip.

I've seen this one for 140,00 (sports check).

Marmot Minimalist €200,00

Gore-TEX paclite. It is completely waterproof and windproof yet is packable and lightweight. It is a 2 layer laminate.

Adjustable hood. Arm pit zips.

I have seen it for 100,00. (Hervis.at)

Soft shell

For running. Should be black or orange.

Rab Kinectic Plus €200,00

Proflex membrane is waterproof and stretchy. It is also decently breathable.

Good for running and cycling around the city. Not so much for skiing.

I have seen it for 120,00 (campz.at)

Gore H5 Windstopper €350,00

Already have seen for €160,00 (campz.at).
